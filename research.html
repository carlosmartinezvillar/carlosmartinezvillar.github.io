Research

My present research focuses on the intersection of neural networks for semantic segmentation and remote sensing. The aim of my most recent project is emphasize convolutional "encoder-decoder" models like U-Net to measure the change in earth features due to climate change.

I currently work out of Dr. Grant Scotts's High-Performance Data-Intensive Computational Systems Lab in the EECS department at the University of Missouri
