Research

My current research focuses on the intersection of convolutional neural networks for semantic segmentation of remote sensing images. Right now I am working on project that focuses on using parsimonious convolutional "encoder-decoder" models like U-Net to measure the change in earth features due to climate change.

I work out of Dr. Grant Scotts's High-Performance Data-Intensive Computational Systems Lab in the EECS department at the University of Missouri
